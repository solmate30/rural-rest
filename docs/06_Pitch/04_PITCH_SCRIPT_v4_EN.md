## S1 · Hook `~15s`

Hi, I'm Suyeon Han from Rural Rest.

There are tourists everywhere.
But deep inside rural villages, houses keep getting abandoned — even after being fixed.

The problem isn't the houses.
**There's no structure to keep them running.**

Rural Rest connects the three parties who can fix this.

---

## S2 · Problem `~15s`

Local governments fix houses. But they have no way to run them.
Operators want to help. But the costs are too high.
Global investors want in. But there's no way to access these assets.

**Because the three parties are never connected.**

---

## S3 · Why Now / Why Korea `~20s`

In rural Korea, there are many vacant houses with very low acquisition costs.
And there is real global demand — travelers coming for K-culture want authentic local experiences.

The Korean government passed a special law to solve the vacant house problem, and tokenized securities guidelines are being introduced.
The RWA market has grown 5x in just three years — now at around $24 billion.

**There is no standard for real estate RWA infrastructure yet.
This is the moment to move first.**

## S4 · Solution `~20s`

When a guest checks out, the escrow is released automatically.

Investor 30%, Operator 30%, Local Government 40%.
**The on-chain program distributes it automatically every month.**

Real cash flow. Not speculation.
