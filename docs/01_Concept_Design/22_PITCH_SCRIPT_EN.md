## [S7] Strategic Advantage (20s)

The most important point — zero CAPEX.
In Italy, Mussomeli reused 500 empty houses — tourists grew 10x, population decline stopped.
Japan gives subsidies for akiya renovation. Korea covers up to 70% of renovation costs.
We only handle operations and on-chain settlement. The municipality's 40% goes back into the village. Costs turn into revenue — a virtuous cycle.

Our revenue comes from booking fees of 10 to 15%, token issuance fees, and B2B SaaS licensing in Phase 2.
