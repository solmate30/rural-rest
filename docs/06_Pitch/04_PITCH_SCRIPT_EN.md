## [S1] Hook

Hi, I'm Suyeon from Rural Rest.

There are over 12 million vacant houses globally,
but most of them are not being used.

Rural Rest turns these empty houses into global stays,
and distributes the revenue on-chain.

---

## [S2] Problem

Governments acquire vacant houses,
but without an operating model, they fall back into disuse.

At the same time, rural travel demand is growing organically on social media.

The assets exist. The demand exists.
What's missing is the operating infrastructure.

---

## [S3] Solution

Rural Rest registers vacant houses as RWAs,
brings in investment, and activates operations.

Then the revenue is split:

Investor, Operator, Municipality —
thirty, thirty, forty.

All in a single transaction.
